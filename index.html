About Us

At Canyon Woodburning Co., we turn simple pieces of wood into timeless works of art. From custom name signs and engraved décor to personalized gifts, each burn is done by hand with care, precision, and creativity.

Serving the Caldwell and Nampa area, we love bringing warmth, craftsmanship, and Idaho pride into every design we make.
